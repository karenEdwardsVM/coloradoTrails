About Trexpedition.

Trexpedition is a utility for finding good trails, understanding more about the world around you, and enjoying the most productive time in nature possible.
We believe that Colorado has a lot to offer, that recreational activities and tourism should be driven not just by the gadgets that people purchase, but by appreciation of the natural world.
