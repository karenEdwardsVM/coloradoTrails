Trexpedition: supporting your brain and local environment.

Trexpedition is an educational tool that seeks to improve your productivity in the workplace by increasing your enjoyment of the outside world. While hiking is normally good stimulus for your brain and body, hiking while identifying plants, animals, and fungi is much better for your brain.
Additionally, tourism is a major industry in Colorado, but also a major danger to our wildlife; badly managed recreation can only persist for a short time, in order to profit from our resources, we need to protect them.
We seek to accomplish that by educating hikers, mountain bikers, and ATV drivers about the landscape around them. The more you know about the utility, beauty, and complexity of the natural world, the better you'll protect it.
